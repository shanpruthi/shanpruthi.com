President @ Product Vision Club

Over the past few years, I've worked closely with a team of 10 to help foster a PM community of over 1,000 students at the University of Waterloo. We've partnered with product teams and Waterloo alumni from companies like Google, Uber, Facebook, and Square to organize everything from workshops to alumni programs. This club has been a huge reason for why I switched into product in the first place and hence, I've really enjoyed taking the role on. Check out our Facebook page and website for more details on the Product Vision club.

Things I've helped organize:

Events & Workshops

Full day speaker events, interactive product work sessions, QnA's, and panels.

Mentorship Programs

Pairing Waterloo alumni in product roles with current students looking to learn more about the role.

Resume Critiques

Helping first and second year students prepare resumes for product applications.

Interview Prep

Helping students prepare for their interviews by holding 1 on 1, personalized mock interview sessions.

Online Resources

Curated education articles and videos for students that want to become product managers.

Podcasts

A new initiative to learn more about local professionals and their path to product.
