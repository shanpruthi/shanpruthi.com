Product Manager @ Yelp

Team Local Services
Location San Francisco, CA
Duration Winter 2018

I had the amazing opportunity to work on an extremely important and fast-moving part of Yelp called Local Services. When people generally think about Yelp, they think about food/restaurants, but services (like movers, plumbers, electricians), have a much larger opportunity for generating revenue. My product focus was on increasing the engagement on our primary feature called "Request a Quote", which let people review and compare prices for the jobs they needed to get done.

It was a lot of fun as I learned everything from building relationships with engineers, running useful experiments, and managing a ton of responsibility.

A few highlights:

Sole PM for a team of 8 engineers. Effectively managed end-to-end product cycles, launched 7 experiments & defined 1 year roadmap.

Founded a feature to let users compare prices across businesses. Led the cross platform launch, unlocking over $12M/year in ad revenue.

Conducted 9 user interviews to better understand customer behaviour, communicating key results with PMs, VPs & engineering managers.
